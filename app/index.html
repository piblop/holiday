Weather
Summer vibes cooling down. Pack for everything — rain and cold nights included. Gumboots and a raincoat.
Car
Big enough for everyone and their bags, no bigger. Coastal roads take anything. Always book stays with parking.
Pace
3 nights max anywhere. One night per small town, max two — each road-trip stop is about an hour from the next.
Rule
Coast road, not the highway. And as many tomato salads as possible while in Basque country.

Still to decide

  1. Bamberg — keep or cut. It fights the route (east, then all the way back west). Keeping it is authentic and half the point; cutting it frees ~3 nights for San Seb and the Spanish coast. Settle this first.
  2. Île de Ré — the soft spot. Priciest, most optional. Drop it and Royan still covers oysters.
  3. Cross-border car fee. France pickup → Bilbao drop usually carries a one-way international charge. Check it, or return the car in France.
