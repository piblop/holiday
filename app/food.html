Example orders — what to actually ask for

The pintxos crawl San Sebastián, any evening

  1. Bar Néstor — put your name down at 19:30 sharp: tomato salad, a slice of tortilla, txuleta to share
  2. Gandarias — almejas a la marinera, solomillo pintxo
  3. La Cuchara de San Telmo — veal cheek in red wine, seared foie
  4. La Viña — cheesecake and a glass of PX to finish

~€40–50 pp with a txakoli at every stop

The sagardotegi feast fixed menu — you don't choose, it arrives

  1. Chorizo cooked in cider
  2. Salt-cod omelette, then fried cod with green peppers
  3. Txuleta, rare, off the coals
  4. Idiazabal cheese with quince paste and walnuts
  5. Cider from the barrel every time someone shouts «txotx!»

set feast €40–50 pp, cider included

The asador lunch Joxe Mari (Orio) or Elkano (Getaria)

  1. Green salad with onion, dressed sharp — nothing else
  2. Whole wild fish by weight: besugo at Joxe Mari, turbot at Elkano — ask what came in today
  3. Let them portion it at the table; chase the cheek meat
  4. Txakoli, then patxaran with the bill

€50–80 pp (Elkano more) — confirm the fish price when ordering

The Rioja table La Vieja Bodega or any Haro asador

  1. Patatas a la riojana (chorizo-potato stew) or menestra of vegetables
  2. Chuletillas al sarmiento — lamb chops over vine cuttings, eaten with fingers
  3. House gran reserva instead of dessert wine — this is the region for it

€40–60 pp with a proper bottle
